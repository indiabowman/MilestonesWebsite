The Development of Computers

1946-ENIAC

ENIAC was developed by John Presper Eckert and John W.Mauchly, ENIAC stands for the Electronic Numerical Integrator and Computer and it was build at the University of Pennsylvania in 1946.
The University described it as the first general-purpose electronic computer, its "operational characteristics" included memory and arithmetic.
